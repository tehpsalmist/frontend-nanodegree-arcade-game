No, No, Abi!

Hey, you! Princess Abi needs your help!
She must retrieve all her toys from the pool and bring them to the rock, without touching any of the yucky bugs in the road.
Can you guide her safely through this task?

Instructions

  • Use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move Abi, or tap the d-pad below.
  • Make your way carefully across the road to pick up the toys one at a time.
  • Bring each toy to the rock to deposit it and free your hands for another toy.
  • Don't touch the bugs, or you'll drop your toy and be forced to restart!
  • See if you can bring back all of the toys without touching any bugs.
  • Not happy with the score? Reset the game to try again.
